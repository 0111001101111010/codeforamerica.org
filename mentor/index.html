Mentor

Code for America pairs passionate technologists with innovative cities to create useful and beautiful civic technology. The CfA Mentorship Program is a central component of our Fellowship, Brigade, and Accelerator experience. Senior professionals guide our program participants as they engage with governments and stretch their skills. If you have any questions, don't hesitate to get in touch.
Mentorship might include:

  • Hosting 'Office Hours' at CfA: Be available on a pre-determined regular basis, usually for 3-4 hours per visit.
  • Mentoring a Fellow, Accelerator founder, or Brigade member: Commit 1-2 hours per month to partner with an individual on a consistent basis.
  • Providing Periodic Advice: Be on call for when issues related to your specific expertise arise.
  • Presenting at a 'CfA Speaker Series' Event: Give a brief talk to Fellows, founders, and other Friends of CfA.
