Lyzi Diamond

Member of

  • 2014 Fellows

Lyzi Diamond is a geographic information systems analyst, technical/educational writer, and front-end developer. Most recently, she worked in GIS/technical writing for the Oregon Department of Geology and Mineral Industries. She also helped organize women-focused tech and map-related events in Portland. Lyzi studied geography at the University of Oregon and has passions for community building, good cartography, riding bikes, watching college football, and playing handbells.
