Beth Harsch is passionate about her community. Her personal mission statement (to make connections, to encourage collaboration and to inspire people to take action) has been her focus. While honing in on those tenets, Beth launched ConnectHelp. Through ConnectHelp, the community is rallying around a few key issues (recidivism, jobs/economy, homelessness, education, youth) to find tangible ways to move forward and improve the community.
