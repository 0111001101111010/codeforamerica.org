Ian Henshaw

Member of

  • Cary, NC Brigade
  • Brigade Captains

Ian is a Managing Partner of Technology Tank; a think tank that provides technology policy and implementation strategies to government agencies, businesses and non-profits. Ian is also the Director of International Development for the Research Triangle Park Area of North Carolina for Twin City Alliance, consulting to match companies for mutual benefit in International Business and is the Owner and President of Oakhaven Research, an IT Consulting and Integration company. Ian serves on the Town of Cary Economic Development Committee, is active with the Cary Chamber of Commerce and several charitable organizations.
