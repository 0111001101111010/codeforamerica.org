Molly McLeod

Member of

  • 2014 Fellows

Molly is a graphic designer and media artist who has worked on board games, documentary films, and community murals. Most recently, she designed print and digital projects for federal public health agencies at CommunicateHealth. At Hampshire College, she designed an interdisciplinary concentration in typography, urban planning, and community-based art. Molly's mission is to empower people improve their communities through design.
