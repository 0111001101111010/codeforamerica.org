Colin Copeland

Member of

  • Durham, NC Brigade
  • Brigade Captains

Colin is a web developer who likes to hack on data. He co-owns Caktus Group, a 17 member custom web app development firm out of North Carolina specializing in Django-powered websites. At Caktus, he gets to work with a great team on projects for a variety of clients, including UNICEF, UNC, and the University of Chicago. After being introduced to open data, he attended a few Open Data Days and eventually met Andy, his Brigade co-captain, and started Code for Durham.
