Bret Fisher

Member of

Bret is the CEO and Co-Founder of Winsitter.com, a startup helping small businesses monitor their servers from anywhere. He's spent 20 years as a technology engineer, software developer, and team leader in everything from enlisted Navy, to civil service, and multiple companies big and small. He lives in Virginia Beach, VA, USA where it's the perfect mix of small-town people-in-flipflops and big-city fun.
