Andrew Hyder

Developer Relations Engineer

Member of

Andrew is an urban designer and software engineer who enjoys hacking on cities, maps, and telling stories with data. Currently serving his 6th year as a Board of Director at Urban Ecology, Hyder has built several school yards, bicycle paths, and neighborhood parks around the Bay Area. Prior to his Fellowship, Hyder was a Open Source Geo Stack Engineer at Urban Mapping where he worked on an online mapping and data API.
