Sam Hashemi

Member of

  • 2014 Fellows

Sam is a user experience researcher and designer, most recently with NASA where he led an effort to redesign the 1990s era systems aboard the International Space Station with modern iPad and iPhone apps. Previously, he worked to create Palm's webOS and TouchPad tablet. He holds a Masters in Human-Computer Interaction from Carnegie Mellon, and can recite several Dr. Seuss books by heart.
