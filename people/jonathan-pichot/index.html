Jonathan Pichot

Member of

Jonathan has a passion for software, cities, communities, and data. He has helped organize his local Drupal and Ruby groups. He teaches beginners how to program at his local coworking space, as well as organizing Friendly Code, the Code for America Brigade in Grand Rapids. He has volunteered with local urbanist projects, including a recent Build a Better Block. He works for Collective Idea, a Ruby-on-Rails consultancy in Holland, Michigan.
