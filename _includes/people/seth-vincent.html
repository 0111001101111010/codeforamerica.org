Seth Vincent

Member of

  • Seattle, UT Brigade
  • Brigade Captains

Seth Vincent writes code, stories, and music. He's an independent programmer, designer and writer, and is a co-organizer of seattle.io (http://seattle.io) and Code for Seattle (http://codeforseattle.org). Seth writes things like Learn.js (http://learnjs.io), a book about javascript, and builds things like crtrdg.js (http://crtrdg.github.io), a toolkit for 2d games at Super Big Tree (http://superbigtree.com).
