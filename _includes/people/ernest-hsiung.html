Ernest Hsiung

Member of

  • Miami, FL Brigade
  • Brigade Captains

Ernie Hsiung is a co-captain of Code for Miami, a volunteer local civic hacking brigade that has launched community projects such as Miami Wiki and the Florida Legislature Tracker. He also organizes the Front-End Developers of Miami MeetUp group. Currently a Software Engineer for RackSpace, he has worked as a Front-End Engineer at Yahoo! and a Developer Evangelist at Ning.

Originally from the San Francisco Bay Area, in a previous life he received multiple accolades for his award-winning personal blog, Little Yellow Different and was co-founder of the Asian-American website 8Asians.com.
