Code for America is helping cities work better for everyone -- and we’re enlisting the people and power of the web to do it.

We bring local governments and the technology community together to tackle pressing civic problems in areas like criminal justice, citizen engagement, hunger. New tools are lowering costs and improving lives -- and we’re transforming the way citizens and governments interact.

Give to CfA

Raising not just our voices, but also our hands.
Learn More

By donating to CfA, you're supporting the Fellows who give a year of their lives to this cause and the volunteers in our Brigades who have rolled up their sleeves to help City Hall. And you're investing in the future of our country by making your government more open, efficient, and responsive.

Whether it’s $20 or $2,000, every contribution is meaningful. If you would like to discuss a major gift, please contact Ashley Meyers at ashley[at]codeforamericaorg.
