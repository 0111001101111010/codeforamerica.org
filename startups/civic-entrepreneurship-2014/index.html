About

The Charlotte Quality of Life Dashboard is a collection of social, crime, physical, economic and environmental indicators designed to help assess the health of neighborhoods in Charlotte and Mecklenburg County. In this webinar, project leaders Tom Warshauer and Rebecca Hefner will share the story behind the quality of life study, the decision and process to make the data more accessible via a dashboard, and explore some examples of interesting ways the community has used the information to build better neighborhoods.

This event is hosted by the Code for America Peer Network, CfA's community of innovators working in local government from over thirty cities and counties. Learn more about the Peer Network and how you can get involved here.
