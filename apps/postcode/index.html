Postcode

Summary

Livehttp://postcode.io
Contact us to work with Postcode

PostCode is a mission driven company that provides technology applications and services to local governments. The group focuses on open source technology and user centered design with the goal to help the public sector operate more efficiently while engaging citizens more effectively. PostCode offers a suite of products including: RecordTrac, a public records request and management system; Promptly a text message reminder app for government services; and CityVoice a place based call-in system for collecting community feedback. The company's consulting capabilities include full service research, design, development, implementation, training, and maintenance for technology solutions.

To check out the app visit postcode.io
