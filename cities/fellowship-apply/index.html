City Application for the 2015 Fellowship

This application is for Code for America’s 2015 fellowship program – an 11-month program where a team of developers, researchers and designers work alongside your staff to solve local government problems. Deadline for Applications: Mar 31, 2014 Midnight PST.

Prepping Your Application

There are a number of things you can do to build a successful application and become a Code for America fellowship city. Check out the fellowship FAQ, review the program timing and check out the alumni city outcomes.

In past years, the most successful candidates have scheduled a quick chat with our government relations director Luke Norris. To contact Luke email luke@codeforamerica.org or call 816.200.8925.
